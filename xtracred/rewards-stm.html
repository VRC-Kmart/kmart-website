Kmart Rewards
How to Apply
  1. Kmart Rewards are earned by making eligible purchases with the Kmart Credit Card issued by Beneficial National Bank USA (BNB USA).

  2. Kmart Cardholders will have the opportunity to earn Kmart Rewards on eligible purchase made at all U.S. Kmart and Super Kmart stores. Kmart Rewards will be awarded as follows:

    $500 - $9993% Rewards
    $1,000 - $1,4994% Rewards
    $1,500 - $1,9995% Rewards
    $2,000 and over6% Rewards

  3. Kmart Rewards will accumulate on an annual basis starting with the first day following the cardholder's October billing cycle date.

  4. Kmart Reward Dollars, in the amount of the Kmart Rewards accumulated, will be issued within four weeks following the cardholder's October billing cycle date, and the corresponding Kmart Rewards will be deducted on the cardholder's October billing cycle date.

  5. Kmart Reward Dollars will not be issued if there is less than 1 Kmart Reward accumulated, in which case the total will be carried over to the following annual period.

  6. Kmart Reward Dollars are redeemable for merchandise only at U.S. Kmart and Super Kmart stores.

  7. Kmart Reward Dollars will be valid for one year from the date they are issued.

  8. Certain merchandise purchases are not eligible for credit toward earning Kmart Rewards. Ineligible items are: food (including candy, beverages and prepared food), alcohol, tobacco products, hunting and fishing licenses, lottery tickets and purchases at Penske Auto Centers.

  9. Kmart Rewards are earned on the net amount of eligible purchases (not including tax) less eligible returns.

  10. Kmart Reward Dollars cannot be used as payment to a credit card account or to make layaway payments. Certain merchandise cannot be purchased with Kmart Reward Dollars: alcohol, tobacco products, fishing and hunting licenses, gift certificates, lottery tickets, prescription drugs and purchases at Penske Auto Centers.

  11. Kmart Reward Dollars and Rewards are not assignable or transferable, and Kmart Reward Dollars will not be replaced if lost or stolen.

  12. BNB USA and Kmart reserve the right to disqualify a cardholder from participation in the Kmart Rewards program (and to eliminate all rewards) without any further obligation.

  13. BNB USA and Kmart reserve the right to add, modify or delete the Terms and Conditions (including terminating the program or eliminating the right to participate) at any time, without notice.
