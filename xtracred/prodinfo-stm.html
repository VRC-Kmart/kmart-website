PRODUCT INFORMATION


What are the benefits of the Kmart Credit Card?

  • Earn up to 6% back on annual eligible purchases through the Kmart Rewards Program
    •
  • Accepted at over 2100 Kmart and Super Kmarts nationwide, including Puerto Rico and Guam
  • No annual fee
  • No minimum purchase required
  • 25 day grace period
  • Competitive variable interest rate
  • Optional credit card insurance protection

What are the minimum eligibility requirements?
  • Must be 18 years or older
  • Must be a resident of the U.S., Guam, Puerto Rico or other U.S. Territories (Canadian residents not eligible at this time)
  • Must have a Social Security Number and a home telephone number.
