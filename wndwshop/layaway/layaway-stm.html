Don't have enough money to buy all those things you want? Want to not spend so much money at one time for back to school or the upcoming Christmas Holiday? Not a problem, take advantage of Kmart's layaway department. For a small service fee to cover storage costs and operating expenses, Kmart will hold your merchandise in our layaway department.

Kmart's layaway plan has the following features:

  • 10 week layaway plan
  • Bi-weekly payment plan that is calculated for you based on 20% of your merchandise total.
  • If the merchandise goes on sale while in layaway....no problem just come back to our layaway department and notify our associate on duty and they will be happy to adjust the item for you.
  • For your convenience, we have 3 easy ways to make payments:
    • at our front checkouts (as long as payments are not late)
    • at our layaway service counter or
    • by mailing it to our layaway department.
  • Should you decide you no longer want the merchandise once you have put it in layaway, we will refund the money you have paid on it less our service fee and a small cancellation fee.

Sounds great doesn't it? Stop by your local Kmart or Super Kmart today for more details on our convenient layaway department.
