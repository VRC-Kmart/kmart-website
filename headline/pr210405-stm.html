VRCHAT KMART PARTNERS WITH PALM PLAZA MALL
Kmart has signed a deal to partner with Palm Plaza to bring a retro aestheric experience to the New Square wing of Palm Plaza Mall.

METAVERSE, VRC., Apr. 3, 2021
In continuation of our BIG Changes campaign we have officially partnered with Palm Plaza Corp. to open our fourth location as an inline tennant at the New Square Mall wing of Palm Plaza Mall.

In the deal, VRChat Kmart agreed to carry Palm Plaza's new line of apparel in our fashion accessories department. Their line of baseball caps, as well as a cardboard cutout display of the Mall's mascots can be found at our main store location.

In a statement on the partnership, Cake of Palm Plaza Corp. said: "I couldn't be more excited for this partnership. As nostalgia and aesthetics are a major focus, VRChat Kmart is often spoken highly of amongst the staff and mallgoers of the Palm Plaza Mall, as it captures the feeling perfectly with it's impressive accuracy and attention to detail (I'm definitely a huge fan myself!). I hope that by providing direct access to both from each facility, Eric and I can expose even more people to the joys of aestheticism and preserving that special feeling you get from places like these."

When asked, Ericirno stated: "I feel this partnerhsip between Palm Plaza [Mall] and VRChat Kmart will benefit the entirety of VRChat. It's opened the door for an amazing opportunity for players to experience levels of nostalgia they never even knew they had, as well as keep the name of Kmart fresh in people's minds."

VRChat Kmart serves VRChat with 3 discount stores and 1 Super Kmart. Kmart stores serve players from all over the world, with most players coming from the United States. With 100+ associates and a Discord server of 1350+, VRChat Kmart is one of the quickest growing virtual reality retail stores.
