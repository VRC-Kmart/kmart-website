KMART 400 DRIVES NASCAR RACING TO KMART EXPRESS

METAVERSE, VRC., July 8th, 2023
Kmart Express is revving up the engines of excitement with the highly anticipated first-annual Kmart 400 race, set to take place in the heart of VRChat. The event promises an adrenaline-fueled spectacle that is guaranteed to draw racing enthusiasts and local residents alike to the Kmart Express parking lot. This thrilling race is not only a celebration of speed and skill but also a driving force behind increased foot traffic to the store.

Scheduled to be held on July 9th, the Kmart 400 race will feature a dynamic lineup of talented drivers, racing their high-performance vehicles around a specially designed "VRC Kmart Speedway" within the Kmart Express parking lot. Spectators will have the opportunity to witness heart-stopping moments, exhilarating overtakes, and intense competition as drivers navigate sharp turns and sprint down straightaways in their quest for victory.

"We are thrilled to bring the Kmart 400 Race to Kmart Express and host such an extraordinary event right here in our own parking lot," said Ryan, CEO of VRC Kmart. "Not only does this race showcase the incredible skill and precision of the participating drivers, but it also provides an unforgettable experience for our customers and the community."

With its strategic location, Kmart Express recognizes the tremendous opportunity to leverage the Kmart 400 race to boost foot traffic and engage with racing enthusiasts. The event is expected to attract a diverse audience, ranging from avid motorsport fans to players seeking a unique and entertaining experience.

"We want the Kmart 400 race to be a night of fun and excitement for everyone involved," added PablosCorner, CCO of VRC Kmart. "Not only will attendees witness an incredible display of skill and speed, but they will also have the opportunity to explore our stores, take advantage of exclusive offers, and immerse themselves in the racing atmosphere."

VRC Kmart encourages all racing enthusiasts and players to mark their calendars and join in the exhilaration of the first-annual Kmart 400 race. The event promises to be a highlight of the season, bringing the community together for an unforgettable experience filled with speed, competition, and the unbeatable atmosphere of motorsports.

For more information and updates about the Kmart 400 race, as well as other exciting events at VRC Kmart, please visit the official Kmart Discord.

VRC Kmart serves VRChat with 2 discount stores and 1 Super Kmart. Kmart stores serve players from all over the world, with most players coming from the United States. With 100+ associates and a Discord server of 4200+, VRC Kmart is one of the quickest growing virtual reality retail stores.
