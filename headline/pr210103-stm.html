KMART EXPRESS LOCATION TEMPORARILY CLOSED DUE TO GAME BREAKING GLITCH
Cross-platform location Kmart Express temporarily shuttered due to major in-game bug.

METAVERSE, VRC., Jan. 3, 2021
On the evening of January 2nd, 2021 a game breaking bug was brought to the attention of Ryan Reynolds that caused both Quest and PC players to crash and was quickly spreading across public instances of Kmart Express.

Investigations are currently pending and the store has been temporarily closed until further notice. Quest associates are asked to please be patient as the VRC Kmart Development Team looks into troubleshooting the issue.

The VRC Kmart main store is unaffected from these bugs, as it is an issue between objects on the PC and Oculus Quest versions of VRChat. We will post updates to the VRC Kmart Discord server on the progress of these fixes and will have more details in the coming days.

VRC Kmart Corporation has no definitive date on when the store will reopen, however it is expected to be reopened within the next few weeks.

VRC Kmart serves VRChat with 2 discount stores and 1 Super Kmart. Kmart stores serve players from all over the world, with most players coming from the United States. With 100+ associates and a Discord server of 1000+, VRC Kmart is one of the quickest growing virtual reality retail stores.



UPDATE, Jan. 16, 2021
Kmart Express has resumed normal daily operations on both the PC and Oculus Quest verisons of the map. If any issues are encountered, please contact either the Kmart Development Team or Ryan Reynolds.
