VRC Kmart OPENS DISTRIBUTION CENTER TO THE PUBLIC

METAVERSE, VRC., March 29th, 2023
VRC Kmart opens a small area of it's Distribution Center to public viewing, allowing shoppers a view into the logistics of how we get their favorite products to the stores. This move is part of our ongoing effort to provide greater transparency and engagement with our customers and the community.

Starting today, visitors will be able to tour a designated section of our state-of-the-art distribution center in VRChat. This section will showcase our advanced inventory management systems, cutting-edge robotics technology, and efficient supply chain management processes.

The public tour is an opportunity for VRC Kmart to showcase our commitment to innovation and our dedication to providing the best customer experience possible. We believe that by opening up our distribution center to the public, we can give customers an inside look at how we operate, and inspire them with our commitment to excellence.

VRC Kmart serves VRChat with 2 discount stores and 1 Super Kmart. Kmart stores serve players from all over the world, with most players coming from the United States. With 90+ associates and a Discord server of 4000+, VRC Kmart is one of the quickest growing virtual reality retail stores. By opening up our distribution center, we hope to demonstrate our commitment to transparency, innovation, and excellence, and to inspire the next generation of innovators and entrepreneurs.

We welcome the public to come and experience our distribution center firsthand, and to learn more about the exciting things happening at VRC Kmart.
