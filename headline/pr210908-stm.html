VRC Kmart OPENS MINI KMART AT VRCON 2021
VRC Kmart Corporation opens Kmart Mini as a booth at VRCon, open September 9th through the 12th.

METAVERSE, VRC., Sept. 8th, 2021
VRC Kmart Corporation is debuting it's second Kmart Mini store at VRCon 2021 from September 9th through the 12th. After weeks of constructing, stocking, and optimizing, we are proud to debut the to the VRChat public.

The Kmart Mini booth location will be down the left wing of the VRCon communities world across from the Loli Police Department's station.

We're hoping this new booth will bring attention to our community, our future store location (Super Kmart Center), and our core purpose.

VRC Kmart associates and managers will be available all weekend at the booth to interact with VRCon attendees and shoppers.
