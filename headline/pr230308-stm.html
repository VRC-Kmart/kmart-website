VRC Kmart ANNOUNCES NEW ASSISTANT STORE MANAGER

METAVERSE, VRC., March 8th, 2023
VRC Kmart is pleased to announce the promotion of Pablo to the position of assistant store manager. With his years of experience and dedication to the company, we are confident that he will excel in his new role.

Starting off as an electronics associate back in 2020, he applied for the Development Team and worked on making Super Kmart Center a (virtual) reality, becomign the lead of the Development Team.

As assistant store manager, Pablo will be assisting in overseeing day-to-day operations, managing inventory, and helping to lead our team of associates and department managers. He has demonstrated a strong work ethic, great management skills with members of the Development Team and Development Support Team, and a great attitude in store to shoppers and fellow coworkers.

"Pablo has been an important member of our team for several years, and we are thrilled to promote him to this new role," said Ryan, Store Manager at VRC Kmart. "His passion for the job, attention to detail, and leadership skills will be essential in ensuring the continued success of our store and making sure we stay hip."

Pablo has a proven track record of delivering excellent results in his previous role as Development Team Manager and is ready to take on the new challenges that come with his promotion. He is excited to lead the team and work towards providing the best shopping experience for our customers.

Please join us in congratulating Pablo on his well-deserved promotion. We are confident that he will make significant contributions to our store in his new role as assistant store manager.

VRC Kmart serves VRChat with 1 discount store, 1 Super Kmart, and 1 Kmart Express gas station. Kmart stores serve players from all over the world, with most players coming from the United States. With 80+ associates and a Discord server of 3900+, VRC Kmart is one of the quickest growing virtual reality retail stores.
