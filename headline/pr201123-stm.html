VRCHAT KMART DEBUTS 2020 BLACK FRIDAY DOORBUSTERS AND EVENT.

METAVERSE, VRC., Nov. 23, 2020 -- VRChat Kmart is announcing it's new Black Friday Doorbuster event and circular for the 2020 holiday season.

Starting at 12 AM EST on Thursday, November 26th all VRChat Kmart stores will be open for our Thanksgiving sale. These sales will continue into the weekend until Cyber Monday. The night of Thanksgiving we will be hosting the official Black Friday "work event" where associates will be staffed at all counters to serve the VRChat public.

Multiple public intances are expected, and we will be ready. Our associates will be available to spread out depending on the popularity of the update.

Coinciding with the new event is a Black Friday circular with over 48+ sales in multiple departments at the Main Store ("VRChat Kmart") and Kmart Express locations.
Our Black Friday 2020 circular can be found on our Twitter page.

Ryan Reynolds was quoted saying: "I can't wait to hear how shoppers react to this update. A lot of love has gone into getting this event ready for the VRChat community. So while the real stores are closed this Thanksgiving, we are ready to show the world why we're 'VRChat's favorite store!'"

Associates will recieve details in the near future about staffing the event, as well as projected traffic patterns in an associate newsletter.
