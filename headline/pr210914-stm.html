VRCHAT KMART MAIN STORE TEMPORARILY CLOSING FOR REMODEL
VRChat Kmart Corporation announces the temporary closure of their main store for much needed major renovations.

METAVERSE, VRC., Sept. 14th, 2021
Today VRChat Kmart Corporation is announcing the beginning of the Super Moving Sale in our main store location. Main store is not permanently closing, it is only closing temporarily for rennovations and remodel.

With the grand opening of Super Kmart Center set in stone on September 22nd, 2021, all remaining merchandise at main store will be sold at heavy discounts throughout the weekend. Remaining fixtures will not be for sale as they will be shipped to Super Kmart Center for reuse.

Rennovations for main store include an upgrade to it's IT systems, improved apparel (avatar) pedestals, realistic lighting, and most importantly of all: Rebuilding the store from the ground up in Blender.

Super Kmart Center will also be open to Oculus Quest at a later date. Once Oculus Quest support is added Kmart Express will close for remodel and renovations as well.

When asked about the closing of main store, PablosCorner, the Development Team Co-Lead, had this to say: "I am a bit sad about main store closing. Got my start doing electronics before I ended up becoming the development lead for Super K, although I am excited for the future of VRChat Kmart with the new opening of Super Kmart Center!"

Ryan Reynolds, CEO, was quoted in saying: "With the goal in mind to streamline operations and create a more inclusive work environment and community where all shoppers and associates can hang out in one Super saving place, I feel that closing main store for a bit will be well worth it once it makes a return as a historically accurate - and optimized - recreation of my Kmart."

VRChat Kmart serves VRChat with 3 discount stores and 1 Super Kmart. Kmart stores serve players from all over the world, with most players coming from the United States. With 300+ associates and a Discord server of 1900+, VRChat Kmart is one of the quickest growing virtual reality retail stores.
