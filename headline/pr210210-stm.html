VRC Kmart WEBSITE AND DATABASES FULLY RESTORED
All sites fully operational after a temporary outtage the night prior.

METAVERSE, VRC., Feb. 10, 2021
On the evening of February 10th, 2021 all VRC Kmart sites and databases went temporarily down due to a website malfunction.

Upon further investigation it appeared to have been a server side issue with our domain host. VRC Kmart is now running on a new domain service and host, providing around the clock service and helping us bring you a great online experience.

The stores were not affected by these site malfunctions and will continue operating as normal. The transfering of databases may have caused some applications to get corrupted. Applicants that have not heard from Human Resources should reapply via our Apply Today form.

VRC Kmart serves VRChat with 2 discount stores and 1 Super Kmart. Kmart stores serve players from all over the world, with most players coming from the United States. With 100+ associates and a Discord server of 1,000+, VRC Kmart is one of the quickest growing virtual reality retail stores.
