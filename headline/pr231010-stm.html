KMART UNVEILS SPINE-TINGLING TRANSFORMATION: WELCOME TO SPOOKY KMART CENTER!

METAVERSE, VRC., October 10th, 2023
VRC Kmart is thrilled to announce the grand unveiling of its spine-tingling transformation of it's current flagship store: "Spooky Kmart Center"! Just in time for Halloween, this ghoulishly revamped Super Kmart Center promises an unforgettable shopping experience for the entire community.

Step into a world where spooky meets savings! The Spooky Kmart Center is designed to transport customers into a Halloween wonderland, with everything from eerie lighting, to chilling decorations and Halloween Muzak that will send shivers down your spine. From the moment you enter, you'll be immersed in the spirit of the season.

While the ambiance may be hair-raising, rest assured that the incredible deals you know and love from Kmart are still at the heart of the Spooky Kmart Center experience. Shoppers can visit the Totally Ghoul Shop and expect to find devilishly good discounts on a wide range of Halloween essentials, including costumes, decorations, party supplies, and more. Whether you're looking to transform into a creature of the night or simply need some candy for those trick-or-treaters, our Spooky Kmart Center has you covered.

In addition to the spooktacular shopping experience, the Spooky Kmart Center will host a series of fang-tastic events and activities throughout the Halloween season. From costume contests to a Halloween party, there will be something for ghouls and goblins of all ages to enjoy. Keep an eye on our website and social media channels for updates on these hair-raising happenings.

"With development of Super Kmart Center more streamlined, it will be easier for us to provide updates and new features at a quicker and easier pace. We look forward to showing what's in the works!"" added PablosCorner, COO of VRC Kmart. Not only is the Spooky Kmart Center update a Spooktacular experience for shoppers, it's also a step for VRC Kmart's Development Team to more streamlined operations on the back end.

The Spooky Kmart Center invites you to embrace the Halloween spirit like never before. Don't miss out on this one-of-a-kind shopping experience that promises to leave you both delighted and spooked.

VRC Kmart serves VRChat with 2 discount stores and 1 Super Kmart. Kmart stores serve players from all over the world, with most players coming from the United States. With 100+ associates and a Discord server of 4400+, VRC Kmart is one of the quickest growing virtual reality retail stores.
