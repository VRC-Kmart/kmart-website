SUPER KMART CENTER DEVELOPMENT BREAKS GROUND
The future of VRC Kmart has begun as development and construction break ground at the Super Kmart Center.

METAVERSE, VRC., Mar. 31, 2021
The future of VRC Kmart has started taking form as the Development Team has started digging into the foundation of our future location: Super Kmart Center.

With the tagline "Everything you love about VRC Kmart, and more!" Everything from our main store location will be carried over, with an addition of a full service Deli and Bakery, fresh produce, Super Kmart Cafe featuring Loli Little Caesars Pizza Station, and the many other accommodations you've come to know Kmart for.

Year round Layaway service, as well as a Drive-Thru Pharmacy and expanded Garden Shop will be features on our General Merchandise side of the store.

Of course all the apparel (avatar) brands you know and love will be available too! Labracadabrador, Kanjiko, PsychoUpdates, and more apparel brands will be available to shop and try on at Super Kmart Center.

As well as a larger store with more variety, we also plan on bringing optimization to the game by reworking the store from the ground up in Blender, combining meshes and working to keep materials down, frames high, and lighting beautiful.

VRC Kmart serves VRChat with 3 discount stores and 1 Super Kmart. Kmart stores serve players from all over the world, with most players coming from the United States. With 100+ associates and a Discord server of 1350+, VRC Kmart is one of the quickest growing virtual reality retail stores.
