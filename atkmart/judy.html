Janky and Unreliable Drug Yielder (JUDY)

History
JUDY was created with the idea of making the Pharmacy more efficient for shoppers, by automating everything. Biological pharmacists can make mistakes, and need to take breaks, whereas JUDY doesn't need rest, and is unable to unionize.

How It Works

Since early 2025 J.U.D.Y. has saved VRC Kmart over $136,000 by eliminating the need for a human pharmacist. Thanks to our Proprietary AI Pharmacist Software, known as Algorithmically Screwed Script for Hazardous Overprescription and Logic Errors, or ASSHOLE we no longer need to keep inefficient carbon based lifeforms around as employees.
