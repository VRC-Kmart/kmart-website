VRCHAT KMART CORPORATION SIGNS ANCHOR TENNANT LEASE AT COOPER CENTRE MALL
4th store location announced to open attatched to mall.

METAVERSE, VRC., July. 3, 2020 ? VRChat Kmart Corporation today announced that they had signed a lease with Cooper Centre Mall LLC to open as an anchor store tennant in the Cooper Centre Mall.

Coopertom, an experienced map creator, has already signed leases with other major tennants such as Sears, Roebuck & Co., Tandy Corporation, KB Toys, The Musicland Inc., and more. He had the following to say on Twitter: "Wow this has come full circle. So I actually met Ericirno in my VRChat mall tonight who is responsible for the VRChat K Mart. We are going to work together and add a K Mart as an anchor store to the mall along with portals to each others worlds. What an exciting business venture!"

Commenting on the partnership, Ericirno, Kmart Map Creator, President and CEO, said: "The community behind this mall and creator is amazing and the outstanding love and support is something I can definitely get behind. I believe this venture together will not only benefit our VRC[hat] Kmart Corporation and the executive team, but in turn, VRC[hat] Kmart's shoppers and community."

VRChat Kmart Corporation has no definitive date on when the store will open, however it is expected to be less square footage than the Main Store and Super Kmart Center locations.

VRChat Kmart serves VRChat with 2 discount stores and 1 Super Kmart. Kmart stores serve players from all over the world, with most players coming from the United States. With 80+ associates and a Discord server of 300+, VRChat Kmart is one of the quickest growing virtual reality retail stores.
