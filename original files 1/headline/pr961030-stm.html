VRCHAT KMART TO IMPLEMENT SELF-CHECKOUTS IN MAIN STORE LOCATION
New self-checkouts to allow customers toring themselves out.

METAVERSE, VRC., May 29, 2020 - VRChat Kmart Corporation came to the final conclusion after recieving multiple suggestions and requests to begin a test phase of cashierless checkouts.

"Through observations in the upcoming week by Kmart associates and management, we will gague the effectiveness and acceptance of these self checkouts. We also have to take into consideration if they bring any additional lag or size increasse to the map." Store manager Ericirno was quoted saying.

Cashierless checkouts does pose an issue to security on making sure merchandise is paid for at the checkouts. The VRChat Kmart Security team has confirmed that cameras will be watching the self-checkout machines 24/7.

Ericirno declined to comment if the self-checkours will be coming to any other Kmart locations.
